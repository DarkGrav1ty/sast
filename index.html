Who We Are

The San Andreas State Troopers are the statewide law enforcement agency focused on highway safety, traffic enforcement, and critical incident response.

Troopers handle high-risk stops, pursuits, crash investigation, DUI enforcement, and support local agencies when backup is needed.

Commissioner’s Message

Every traffic stop and every call reflects the badge you wear. Carry yourself with discipline, respect, and courage. The people of San Andreas deserve nothing less.
